    74HC123 Dual Retriggerable Monostable Multivibrator — Example Circuit
    ======================================================================

    Supply: VCC = 5 V (pin top-centre), GND (pin bottom-centre).
    All logic thresholds and output levels track the connected supply.

    A 1 Hz square-wave clock drives both sections:

      Section 1 — LEADING-EDGE trigger (B input)
        • 1Ā  = GND (LOW)  → B trigger always armed
        • 1B  = 1 Hz clock → rising edge fires a 450 ms pulse on 1Q
        • 1R̄  = 5 V (HIGH) → reset inactive

      Section 2 — TRAILING-EDGE trigger (Ā input), with manual Reset
        • 2Ā  = 1 Hz clock → falling edge fires a 900 ms pulse on 2Q
        • 2B  = 5 V (HIGH) → Ā trigger always armed
        • 2R̄  = logic-input button (starts HIGH = inactive)
          → click button to drive LOW and immediately cancel the pulse

    Pulse widths:
        tW₁ = 0.45 × 100 kΩ × 10 µF = 450 ms   (Section 1)
        tW₂ = 0.45 × 100 kΩ × 20 µF = 900 ms   (Section 2)

    Pin post coordinates (chip x1=432, y1=160, cspc2=32, sizeX=4, sizeY=7):
      W-side  x=432  →  1Ā(y=160) 1B(y=192) 1R̄(y=224) | 2Ā(y=288) 2B(y=320) 2R̄(y=352)
